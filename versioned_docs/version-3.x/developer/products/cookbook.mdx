### Custom price calculation

### Linking products or creating recommendation

Here is an example of linking products to create _Similar products_ feature.

1. In `Configuration` create attribute `Similar products` of type `References` and `Entity`.
2. Assign `Similar products` attribute to desired `Product type` on product level.
3. Products will now show `Similar fields` to link other products.
