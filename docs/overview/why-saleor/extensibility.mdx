## Technology-agnostic

Saleor is extensibility model is designed around GraphQL and API only approach, there is no exclusivity or preference to any technology.
We follow [Open Standards](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_standard) approach.

### Benefits

• **Diverse talent pool** Technology does not limit you to a group of experts.

• **Works with your existing stack** You are not dependent on technology choices of your platform.

• **Pick best tools for the job**
