### Our approach to open source

• **We mean business.** Our core, dashboard, apps, starter kits, docs, everything is open-source and has free licence.

• **Single version of the product.** There is **no** enterprise edition and Community edition. We want to put the best tools in the hands of every business.

• **Centrally governed.** We are open to contributions but we have centralized decision making process which project predictable and have focused vision.
